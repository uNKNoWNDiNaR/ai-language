**Principles**
- One concept per question.
- 12 words or fewer per question.
- Calm, simple, everyday language.
- acceptedAnswers includes punctuation and contraction variants.
- conceptTag format: `a1.<topic>.<subtopic>`.

**Roadmap**
| Lesson ID | Title | Goals | Grammar Focus | Sample conceptTags |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| basic-1 | Greetings & introductions | Say hello, ask names, simple greetings, say goodbye | Present simple greetings and questions | `a1.greetings.hello`, `a1.greetings.name` |
| basic-2 | Pronouns + "to be" | Short identity sentences and simple questions | I am/you are/he is/she is; contractions; basic questions | `a1.be.i_am`, `a1.be.contraction_im` |
| basic-3 | Present simple (I/you/we/they) | Talk about likes, wants, and possessions | Present simple base form; and; simple objects | `a1.present.like`, `a1.present.have` |
| basic-4 | Present simple (he/she/it) | Describe others with third person | Third person -s; does/doesn't | `a1.present.he_likes`, `a1.present.doesnt` |
| basic-5 | Questions (Do/Does + W-words) | Ask where, what, and name review | Do/Does; where/what/who | `a1.questions.do_you`, `a1.questions.where_live` |
| basic-6 | There is/are + articles | Describe common nouns and quantity | there is/there are; a/an/the | `a1.there_is.singular`, `a1.articles.a_an` |
| basic-7 | Possessives + family | Talk about family and belongings | my/your/his/her; simple descriptions | `a1.possessive.my`, `a1.family.mother` |
| basic-8 | Time & routines | Describe daily routines and frequency | at/on; simple time; adverbs of frequency | `a1.time.at_7`, `a1.routine.every_day` |
| basic-9 | Can/can't | Talk about ability and simple requests | can/can't; short requests | `a1.can.ability`, `a1.can.request` |
| basic-10 | Imperatives + directions | Give simple commands and directions | imperatives; left/right | `a1.imperative.sit`, `a1.directions.left` |
| basic-11 | Food & drink | Order politely and express wants | I'd like; please/thank you | `a1.food.like`, `a1.polite.please` |
| basic-12 | Review lesson | Short dialogue-style practice across all prior concepts | Mixed review | `a1.review.greetings`, `a1.review.questions` |

**Definition Of Great**
- Repetition across lessons to reinforce core structures.
- Spaced review hooks via conceptTag reuse.
- Minimal confusion: one concept per question.
- Calm tone always.
