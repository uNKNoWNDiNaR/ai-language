## LESSON BLUEPRINT (A1 English track)

## Question rules:
- One concept per question.
- A1 sentences: max 10–12 words.
- No subordinate clauses (because/although) in early lessons.
- Prefer present simple before past/future.
- Avoid rare vocab; reuse common words often.

## Required fields per question:
- id (number)
- conceptTag (string)
- question (prompt)
- answer (canonical)
- acceptedAnswers (must include answer)
- hints (0–2 short nudges)

## Allowed question types (pick 1 per lesson):
1) Say: “…” (translation/production)
2) Complete: “…” (fill blank)
3) Choose the correct sentence (minimal)

## Hint style:
- short, specific (“Start with: I am …”)
- never shame/rush

## Explanations:
- only needed for forced advance or summary later
- keep 2–4 lines max

ConceptTag format:
a1.<topic>.<subtopic>  e.g. a1.greetings.hello
